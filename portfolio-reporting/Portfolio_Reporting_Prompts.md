# Portfolio Reporting Prompts for PMs

Prompts to generate clear, executive-ready reporting using AI tools.

---

## 1. Status Summary Generation
**Prompt:**  
“Generate a concise, executive-ready status summary for this project. Include progress, risks, blockers, and next steps.”

---

## 2. KPI Reporting
**Prompt:**  
“Create a KPI dashboard narrative based on these metrics. Highlight trends, risks, and recommended actions.”

---

## 3. Risk & Issue Reporting
**Prompt:**  
“Summarize current risks and issues. Include severity, impact, mitigation status, and escalation recommendations.”

---

## 4. Portfolio Roll-Up
**Prompt:**  
“Combine these project updates into a single portfolio-level summary. Highlight cross-project dependencies and risks.”

---

## 5. Executive Briefing Prep
**Prompt:**  
“Prepare a briefing outline for executives covering achievements, challenges, decisions needed, and upcoming milestones.”

---

These prompts help PMs deliver clear, consistent, and strategic reporting.
