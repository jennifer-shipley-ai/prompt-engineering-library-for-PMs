# Requirements Gathering Prompts for PMs

A curated set of prompts to accelerate requirements discovery, clarification, and alignment using AI tools.

---

## 1. Eliciting Business Requirements
**Prompt:**  
“Act as a business stakeholder. Describe the outcomes you expect from this solution, the problems it should solve, and how success should be measured.”

---

## 2. Clarifying Ambiguous Requirements
**Prompt:**  
“Here is a requirement: <insert requirement>. Identify ambiguities, assumptions, missing details, and risks. Rewrite it to be clear, testable, and actionable.”

---

## 3. Technical Requirements Translation
**Prompt:**  
“Translate these business requirements into technical requirements suitable for engineering teams. Include data needs, integration points, constraints, and acceptance criteria.”

---

## 4. Requirements Prioritization
**Prompt:**  
“Given this list of requirements, categorize them into Must‑Have, Should‑Have, Could‑Have, and Won’t‑Have (MoSCoW). Provide rationale for each.”

---

## 5. Risk-Based Requirements Review
**Prompt:**  
“Review these requirements and identify potential risks related to data, compliance, security, and user adoption. Suggest mitigations.”

---

These prompts help PMs accelerate clarity, alignment, and delivery readiness.
